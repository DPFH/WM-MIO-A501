Insert Text Into Paint

1. Select the "Text" button on toolbar.


2. Click down and drag on canvas to get a rectangle on paint, then release.


3. HandyPaint will create a Text Bar on paint as following:


4. You can edit text in the Text Bar. You can resize the Text Bar to scale the text in it. You can also move the Text Bar to change the position that text will be displayed on your paint. By clicking on the triangle that at top-left of Text Bar, you will get a menu of text style to set text Bold, Italic, Underline or Strikeout.


5. You can click on the canvas anywhere outside the Text Bar to complete editing text. Text will be displayed on your paint.
