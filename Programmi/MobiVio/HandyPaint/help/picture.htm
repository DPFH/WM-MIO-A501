Insert Picture Into Paint

1. Click the "Insert Picture" button on toolbar. Open Picture Dialog will be displayed. You can select a picture in the dialog to insert into your paint.


2. After selecting picture to open, HandyPaint will create a Image Bar on paint as following:


3. You can resize the Image Bar to scale the picture in it. You can also move the Image Bar to change the position that picture will be displayed on your paint. By clicking on the triangle that at top-left of Image Bar, you will get a option menu for picture stretching style:

    - Constrain Proportions. If you check up this option, the picture's proportions will be constrained when you scale it.

    - Actual Size. Use this option to display the picture as its original size.


4. You can click on the canvas anywhere outside the Image Bar to complete picture inserting. The picture will be displayed on your paint.
