Save Paint As

You can also save the current drawing paint as BMP, JPG, PNG or GIF format image file.


Click the "Save As" button on Command Panel. Save As Dialog will be displayed. You can specify the Name, Location and Type to save. After saved ok, HandyPaint will launch the device default opener to open the saved image automatically for your further using, such as send to your friend or set as wallpaper.
