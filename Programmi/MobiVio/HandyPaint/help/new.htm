Create New Paint

1. Click the "New" button on Command Panel;


2. HandyPaint provides 4 types of new paint for you to create:

    (1) Empty Paint. This type will lead you to create en empty paint. You can specify canvas size and canvas color for the new empty paint.

    (2) Open Picture. This type will lead you to open an existing picture on your device as your new paint canvas.

    (3) Take a Picture. This type will launch the camera on your device, so you can take a picture as your new paint canvas.

    (4) Take a Screenshot. This type will show waiting (for 10 seconds) dialog, so screen will be captured in 10 seconds. HandyPaint takes the screenshot as your new paint canvas.
