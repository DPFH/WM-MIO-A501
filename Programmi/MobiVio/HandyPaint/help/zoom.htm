Zoom Paint

If you select the "Zoom" button on toolbar, Zoom Bar will be displayed. There are several commands on Zoom Bar:

    - Zoom in. Use this command to stretch your paint.

    - Zoom out. Use this command to shrink your paint.

    - Whole Canvas. Auto stretch/shrink your paint to be displayed entirely in screen.

    - Canvas Width. Auto fit the paint width to screen.

    - 25%, 50%, 100%, 200%, 400%. Stretch/Shrink your paint as the specified percentage.

    - Hide. Use this command to hide Zoom Bar. You can also hide Zoom Bar by clicking the "Zoom" button on toolbar again.
